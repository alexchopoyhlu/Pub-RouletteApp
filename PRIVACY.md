# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** June 25, 2026  
**Last Updated:** June 25, 2026

## Introduction

Pub Roulette ("we," "us," "our," or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our iOS mobile application.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our App.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide Directly

**Player Name**
- You provide your name when creating or joining a party
- Your name is visible to other players in the same party
- We use this to display team assignments and leaderboards

**Party Information**
- Party codes (6-character alphanumeric identifiers)
- Number of teams and pubs
- Drink preferences and selections
- Custom pub additions or modifications

### 1.2 Location Information

**Device Location**
- **When collected:** Only when you start a game (if not using custom location)
- **How it's used:** To search for nearby pubs within your specified radius
- **Storage:** Location is NOT permanently stored on our servers
- **Duration:** Used only during pub search; discarded after results are obtained
- **Permission:** Requires "While Using the App" permission from Settings

**Custom Location**
- If you provide a custom search location instead of device location, we use only that coordinates temporarily for the pub search

### 1.3 Photo Data

**Photo Submissions**
- When you submit photos as proof of visiting pubs
- Photos are compressed for upload efficiency
- Photos are stored temporarily in Firebase for team verification
- Only your team members can view your submissions
- Photos are NOT used for any other purpose
- You retain full ownership of your photos

### 1.4 Usage Analytics

**Firebase Analytics** (if enabled)
- Basic app usage data (app opens, feature usage)
- Crash reports for debugging purposes
- Device information (iOS version, device model)
- NO personal identifying information is collected through analytics

### 1.5 Device Information

**Automatically Collected**
- iOS version
- Device model (iPhone 12, iPhone 14 Pro, etc.)
- Unique device identifiers (if necessary for game synchronization)
- Approximate location (for pub search only)

## 2. How We Use Your Information

### Game Functionality
- Display your name and team assignment to other players
- Track team progress and scores
- Manage party creation and joining
- Search for nearby pubs based on your location
- Verify photo submissions from your team
- Synchronize game state in real-time across devices

### Communication
- Send in-app notifications about game events
- Display messages in the party feed
- Notify when team members complete pubs

### Service Improvement
- Analyze crash reports to fix bugs
- Monitor app performance
- Improve user experience

### Legal & Safety
- Comply with legal requests from authorities
- Detect and prevent fraud or abuse
- Enforce our Terms of Service

## 3. Data Storage & Retention

### Where Data is Stored
- **Primary Server:** Google Firebase Firestore (Cloud)
- **Location:** US-based data centers (Google Cloud default)
- **Security:** Encrypted in transit and at rest

### Retention Policy

| Data Type | Retention Period | Why |
|-----------|-----------------|-----|
| Player Name & Team Info | Until party is abandoned | Required for active games |
| Photos | 30 days or until deleted by you | Team verification; then deleted |
| Party Messages | Until party is abandoned | Chat history for the game |
| Completion Records | Indefinitely | Leaderboard history |
| Location Data | Not stored permanently | Used only for real-time pub search |
| Crash Reports | 30 days | Debugging; automatically deleted |
| Usage Analytics | 90 days | Performance monitoring |

### Data Deletion
- You can delete photos at any time during a game
- Party data is retained indefinitely but can be abandoned
- Request permanent data deletion by emailing us

## 4. Who We Share Your Information With

### We Do NOT Share Data With
- Third-party advertisers
- Data brokers or marketing companies
- Social media platforms
- Any external services without your explicit consent

### We DO Share Data With
- **Other Players in Your Party:** Your name, team, progress, submitted photos
- **Google Firebase:** For data storage and real-time synchronization
- **Service Providers:** Only if necessary for app functionality
- **Legal Authorities:** If required by law

### Firebase Data Processing
Google processes data on our behalf. Google's privacy practices are subject to their [Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

## 5. Your Privacy Rights

### Access Your Data
- You can view your player profile and party information in-app
- Contact us to request all data we hold about you

### Correct Your Data
- Edit your player name in settings
- Modify custom pub information
- Update preferences

### Delete Your Data
- Delete your submitted photos from the app
- Request complete account/party deletion by contacting us
- Your data will be deleted within 30 days of request

### Opt-Out of Analytics
- Disable analytics in your device Settings under Privacy
- This prevents usage data collection (does not affect gameplay)

## 6. Location Privacy

### Permission Requirements
- Pub Roulette requests "When Using the App" location permission
- This is required only to search for nearby pubs
- Location permission is NOT required if using custom location or manually-added pubs

### Location Use
- **Searched:** Only when host starts a game
- **Transmitted:** Only to find nearby pubs (no tracking)
- **Stored:** NOT stored permanently on our servers
- **Shared:** NOT shared with other players or third parties

### Disabling Location
- Go to **Settings** → **Privacy** → **Location Services**
- Toggle Pub Roulette off (you can still play using custom locations)
- Host can manually add pubs instead of searching

## 7. Camera & Photo Privacy

### Camera Permission
- Required only to submit photos as evidence
- Used only when YOU initiate photo capture
- NOT used for continuous monitoring or background access

### Photo Handling
- Photos are compressed to reduce file size
- Stored only in Firebase for team verification
- Only visible to your team members
- You can delete photos anytime
- No facial recognition or image analysis performed

## 8. Security Measures

### Data Protection
- **Encryption in Transit:** SSL/TLS encryption for all data transfers
- **Encryption at Rest:** Firebase provides encrypted storage
- **Access Control:** Authentication required to access party data
- **No Passwords:** We don't store passwords (no account-based authentication)

### What We're Not Responsible For
- Loss of data due to user action
- Unauthorized access if someone else has your party code
- Third-party security vulnerabilities
- Data shared voluntarily with other players

### Report Security Issues
If you discover a security vulnerability, please email us immediately at alexalichopoyhlu@gmail.com with details.
