Introduction

Pub Roulette ("we," "us," "our," or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our iOS mobile application.

Please read this carefully. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our App.

1. Information We Collect

ℹ️ Information You Provide

  • Player Name: Visible to other players in your party
  • Party Code: 6-character identifier to join games
  • Game Settings: Number of teams, pubs, drink preferences
  • Custom Pubs: Pub names and locations you manually add

📍 Location Information

  • Device Location: Only used to search for nearby pubs, NOT permanently stored
  • Custom Location: If you provide a location instead of device location
  • Permission: Requires "While Using the App" setting

📷 Photo Data

  • Compressed when submitted for efficiency
  • Visible only to your team members
  • Stored temporarily for verification
  • You retain full ownership

📊 Analytics & Device Info

  • iOS version and device model
  • App crash reports (for debugging)
  • Basic usage analytics
  • NO personal identifying information

2. How We Use Your Information

Game Functionality

  • Display your name and team assignment
  • Track team progress and scores
  • Manage party creation and joining
  • Search for nearby pubs
  • Verify photo submissions
  • Synchronize game state in real-time

Communication & Notifications

  • Send in-app notifications about game events
  • Display party feed messages
  • Notify team members of progress

Service Improvement

  • Analyze crash reports to fix bugs
  • Monitor performance
  • Improve user experience

Legal & Safety

  • Comply with legal requests
  • Detect and prevent fraud
  • Enforce Terms of Service

3. Data Storage & Retention

Where Your Data is Stored

  • Server: Google Firebase Firestore
  • Location: US-based data centers
  • Security: Encrypted in transit and at rest

How Long We Keep Your Data

Data Type Retention Period Reason
Player Name & Team Info Until party is abandoned Required for active games
Photos 30 days or until deleted Team verification only
Party Messages Until party is abandoned Chat history
Completion Records Indefinitely Leaderboard history
Location Data Not stored permanently Used only for real-time search
Crash Reports 30 days Debugging & auto-deletion

Delete Your Data

  • Delete photos anytime during a game
  • Abandon party to remove from active list
  • Email us for permanent complete data deletion

4. Who We Share Your Information With

🔴 We DO NOT Share With

  • Advertisers or marketing companies
  • Data brokers
  • Social media platforms
  • Any external services without consent

🟢 We DO Share With

  • Other Players: Your name, team, progress, photos (in your party)
  • Google Firebase: For data storage and real-time sync
  • Legal Authorities: Only if required by law

Google's Privacy: Refer to Google's Privacy Policy for their data practices.

5. Your Privacy Rights

🔍 Access Your Data

  • View your player profile in-app
  • Contact us to request all data we hold

✏️ Correct Your Data

  • Edit your player name
  • Modify custom pub information
  • Update preferences

🗑️ Delete Your Data

  • Remove your photos anytime
  • Request complete deletion by email
  • We'll delete within 30 days

📊 Opt-Out of Analytics

  • Go to device Settings → Privacy
  • Disable analytics (gameplay not affected)

📍 Disable Location Tracking

  • Settings → Privacy → Location Services
  • Toggle Pub Roulette off
  • Use custom locations or manually add pubs instead

6. Location Privacy

✅ What We Do With Your Location

  • Search for pubs: Only when you start a game
  • Temporary use: Location is NOT permanently stored
  • No tracking: We don't track you over time
  • Not shared: Other players don't see your location

❌ What We Don't Do

  • Store your location on our servers
  • Share location with third parties
  • Track you continuously
  • Use location for advertising

7. Camera & Photo Privacy

📷 Camera Permission

  • Required only to take photos as evidence
  • Used only when YOU initiate photo capture
  • NOT used for background monitoring

🖼️ Photo Handling

  • Compressed for upload efficiency
  • Stored only for team verification
  • Visible only to your team
  • You can delete anytime
  • NO facial recognition or image analysis

8. Security Measures

🔐 How We Protect Your Data

  • Encryption: SSL/TLS for all data transfers
  • Storage: Firebase provides encrypted storage
  • Access Control: Authentication required for party data
  • No Passwords: We don't store passwords (no account required)

⚠️ What We're Not Responsible For

  • Data loss due to user actions
  • Unauthorized access if others have your party code
  • Third-party security vulnerabilities
  • Data shared voluntarily with other players

🚨 Security Incident?

Email us immediately at alexalichopoyhlu@gmail.com with details.
